You vs Everyone

Delete Account & Data

Last updated: May 15, 2026

This page explains how to delete your You vs Everyone account and app data.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open the You vs Everyone app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone.
  4. Tap Delete Account Data.
  5. Confirm deletion.

If direct deletion fails, use Request Account Deletion from the same section.

Request deletion by email

You can also request deletion by emailing: youvseveryone.app@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The Google account email used to sign in, if applicable.
  • A short message saying you want your You vs Everyone account/data deleted.

What data may be deleted

  • Your app profile data.
  • Your saved progress where associated with your account.
  • Your account deletion request record after processing.

What may be retained

Some anonymous, aggregated, or security-related records may be retained where needed for app integrity, abuse prevention, analytics, backups, legal compliance, or operational records.

Processing time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days.
