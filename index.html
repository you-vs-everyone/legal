You vs Everyone

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

You vs Everyone (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) provides interactive comparison questions, social insights, streak tracking, and related features.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Question answers and selections
  • Optional profile information such as age range, gender, or country
  • Authentication information when signing in with Google
  • App activity such as streaks, completed questions, achievements, and engagement
  • Device and diagnostic information including crash logs and analytics events
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data

How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality and personalized experiences
  • Display aggregated comparison insights and statistics
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Analyze usage trends and engagement
  • Prevent abuse and maintain platform integrity
  • Serve advertisements and measure ad performance

Authentication

Users may sign in using Google Sign-In through Firebase Authentication. Authentication providers may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage, diagnose crashes, and improve stability and performance.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob or other advertising partners. Advertising providers may collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location data, and usage information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user settings, region, and platform requirements.

Notifications

If enabled, the app may send notifications related to streaks, reminders, challenges, or app activity. Users can disable notifications at any time through their device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Certain data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers including Firebase, Google Sign-In, Google AdMob, analytics providers, and infrastructure providers solely for app functionality, analytics, crash reporting, authentication, and advertising services.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce policies.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect user information, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time
  • You may uninstall the app to stop data collection
  • You may disable notifications through device settings
  • You may reset advertising identifiers through device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
