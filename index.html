Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

You vs Everyone is a mobile app that lets users answer fun comparison questions, see aggregated results, unlock badges, track streaks, and discover personality-style insights.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Answers to app questions
  • Optional profile information such as age range, gender, and country
  • Progress data such as streaks, badges, and daily challenge activity
  • Basic app usage data used to improve the app experience

Optional Profile Data

Optional profile information is used to provide better aggregated insights, such as comparing answers by age range or country. You may skip this information.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Show aggregated comparison results
  • Provide deeper insights
  • Track streaks, badges, and daily challenge progress
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Results shown in the app are aggregated and are not intended to personally identify individual users.

Advertising

The app may show ads, including rewarded ads and interstitial ads. Ad providers may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app may send reminders related to daily challenges, streaks, or app activity. You can disable notifications in your device settings or within the app.

Data Storage

App data may be stored using Firebase services. Some app data may also be stored locally on your device to improve speed and reduce repeated loading.

Children

This app is not intended for children under 13. Users under 13 should not use the app or provide information.

Contact

For questions, feedback, or privacy requests, contact: youvseveryoneapp@gmail.com

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
